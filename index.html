The iRobot Roomba i7 Vacuum Is Now Discounted $100

A great robotic vacuum can transform your cleaning routine. With a robovac, you may never have to clean your floors again—you'll simply come home to a clean house. For a limited time, one of the best robotic vacuums is on sale. The iRobot Roomba i7 is powerful, well-designed, and it's available for $100 off its normal price—reduced to $699.

The iRobot Roomba i7 has a lot going for it. For starters, it's well-designed, coming in a sleek black color that should look great in any home. On top, there's a basic clean button, so if you don't want to control it in other ways, you can always press that button manually.

iRobot Roomba i7 7150

iRobot

Of course, the vacuum is super smart—so you may never need to use that physical button. For example, there's an app that you can use not only to start the cleaning process, but also to schedule it. That means that you can set the vacuum to clean when you're at work, so you'll never actually see (or hear) it clean. The device also works with Alexa and Google Assistant, so you can program the cleaning using voice controls.

The iRobot Roomba i7 is also quite powerful. The vacuum is able to map your home, and can adapt to changes in your flooring. It has a three-stage cleaning system and can clean both carpets and hardwood floors. Further, the vacuum has a high-efficiency filter that enables it to trap as much as 99 percent of cat and dog allergens, so it's perfect for those who have a pet (or friends who are allergic to one).

Shop Now

iRobot Roomba i7+

iRobot

Perhaps you're willing to spend a little more on something even more powerful, in which case consider the iRobot Roomba i7+. The i7+ offers many of the same features as the i7, but it comes with a dirt disposal bin that the vacuum can put dirt in after cleaning—and that bin can hold 30 vacuum bins. That means that you'll rarely have to worry about cleaning out the vacuum. The vacuum is a little more expensive than the standard i7, but for many it will be well worth the extra expense.
